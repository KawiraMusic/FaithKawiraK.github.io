Taarab

Taarab is a swahili folksong sung by the swahili people found in the coastal region of Kenya. This song talks about those people who are jealous and envious of other people. It was performed in 2023 Subcounty Music Festivals. The performers are students from Loreto Kiambu Girls High School. It was one of the best experiences in my music career. The item proceeded to the next level (County Music Festivals).

Qaswida

Qaswida is a muslim song sang during prayers to Allah in Islam and swahili languages. It comprises of different short choruses sung together. They are usually sung in a prayerful mood and the dances are not as vigorous as an African folksong. It was performed during the 2023 Subcounty Music Festivals. The performers are students from Loreto Kiambu Girls High School. The item proceeded to the next level (County Music Festivals).
